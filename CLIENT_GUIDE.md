# 📖 Client User Guide: Scheduling Platform

Welcome to your new Calendly-inspired scheduling platform! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to manage your professional calendar and start accepting bookings today.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

When you first log in, you'll land on your **Dashboard**. From here, you can manage your event types, see your upcoming meetings, and configure when you're available to work.

---

## 🏢 1. Managing Event Types

Event Types represent the different kinds of meetings you offer (e.g., "15-Minute Intro Call" or "1-Hour Strategy Session").

1.  **View Your Events**: On the main Dashboard, you'll see a list of all your active event types.
2.  **Create a New Event**: Click the **"New Event Type"** button in the top-right corner.
    *   **Name**: Give your meeting a clear, professional name.
    *   **Slug**: This is the unique part of the URL (e.g., `discovery-call`). Your link will look like `/[username]/discovery-call`.
    *   **Duration**: Select how long the meeting should last.
3.  **Delete an Event**: Use the **Trash Icon** on any event card if you no longer want to offer that specific meeting type.
4.  **Copy Link**: Click the **"Copy Link"** button to instantly grab the URL for that specific event to share with your clients.

---

## 🕙 2. Setting Your Availability

Before clients can book you, you need to tell the platform when you're working.

1.  **Navigate to Availability**: Click on the **"Availability"** tab in the sidebar.
2.  **Toggle Days**: Use the checkboxes to enable or disable specific days of the week.
3.  **Set Hours**: For each active day, enter your **Start Time** and **End Time**. 
    *   *Tip: Use 24-hour format or follow the time picker instructions.*
4.  **Save Changes**: Don't forget to click **"Save Changes"** at the bottom of the page!

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The system automatically prevents double-bookings. If you already have a confirmed meeting during your available hours, that specific slot will be hidden from your clients.

---

## 🗓️ 3. Managing Meetings

Track all your upcoming and historical appointments in one place.

1.  **Navigate to Meetings**: Click on the **"Meetings"** tab in the sidebar.
2.  **Tabs**:
    *   **Upcoming**: Shows all meetings scheduled for the future.
    *   **Past**: A history of all meetings that have already taken place.
3.  **Cancellations**: If you need to cancel an upcoming meeting, locate it in the list and click **"Cancel Meeting"**.

---

## 🔗 4. Sharing With Clients

Sharing your calendar is simple:

-   **Your Profile Page**: Share `/[username]` (e.g., `/demo`) to show your clients a full menu of all your public event types.
-   **Direct Event Link**: Share a specific link `/[username]/[event-slug]` if you want them to book a specific meeting type immediately.

---

## ✨ 5. The Client Experience

When a client visits your link, they will follow these three simple steps:
1.  **Pick a Date**: They'll see a professional calendar with your available days highlighted.
2.  **Select a Time**: They'll choose from the available 15/30/60 minute slots based on your configuration.
3.  **Confirm Details**: They'll enter their Name and Email. Once they click **Schedule**, both of you are confirmed!

---

## 💡 Pro-Tips for Success
-   **Buffer Time**: If you need a break between meetings, ensure you factor that into your overall availability or event durations.
-   **Consistency**: Keep your availability updated weekly to ensure clients see the most accurate slots.
-   **Public Page**: Regularly check your public profile page to see how it looks from your client's perspective!
